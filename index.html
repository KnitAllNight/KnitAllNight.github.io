Knitting and Quilting

My primary craft is knitting. I've been knitting for 15 years and it's something I do almost every day. I love to learn new techniques and nothing is too difficult!

I always have a few projects on the needles. Preferably, one easy project to take with me anywhere and a more intricate project that doesn't leave the house. The easy project right now is a pair of socks for my son. His feet are bigger than mine, so they won't be a quick knit, but right now it's just knitting in the round with some increases. My intricate project is a two-color brioche shawl. It's so smooshy! Brioche is probably my favorite fabric and technique. Fun to knit, fun to smoosh.

Another one of my crafts is quilting. I'm making a twin+ size quilt in just 2 fabrics, a blue and a yellow. The pattern looks like interlocking square chains. Not long ago I finished a reversible quilt. Each quilt square is made and with both the front and the back at the same time. Typically, an entire quilt top is sewn then it's sandwiched with batting and backing and finally quilted. Quilting each square was much easier since it's small, but assembling them was trickier. But the finished project is really nice!

About me
